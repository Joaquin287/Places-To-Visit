How to Get There

The best way to travel to Japan is by air. Major international airports include:

  • Tokyo: Narita International Airport (NRT) & Haneda Airport (HND)
  • Osaka: Kansai International Airport (KIX)
  • Fukuoka: Fukuoka Airport (FUK)

From the airport, you can take the **Shinkansen (bullet train)** or **local transit** to explore different cities.

Must-Visit Places in Japan

  • Tokyo: Shibuya Crossing, Akihabara, and Tokyo Tower
  • Kyoto: Fushimi Inari Shrine and Arashiyama Bamboo Forest
  • Osaka: Dotonbori Street and Osaka Castle
  • Nara: Friendly deer park and Todai-ji Temple
